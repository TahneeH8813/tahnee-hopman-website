Selected Work

Where the work has run

A working list rather than a highlight reel. Some of it is public and linkable, some of it is the quieter kind of editing that never has a byline.

EnergyQuest · Editor, ongoing

EnergyQuarterly and industry reports

Technical and analytical editing for EnergyQuest's quarterly reports and multi-client publications on the Australian energy sector, covering oil, gas, LNG and the broader energy market. The readership is industry leaders, investors and government, people who will notice a number that doesn't reconcile between the executive summary and table four.

RSL Victoria · Editorial and Communications Lead, 2023–2025

Mufti, the ANZAC Appeal and Beyond the Barracks

Ran editorial and communications for RSL Victoria's member magazine Mufti through a redesign and digital transformation, alongside the website, Annual Report and all content supporting ANZAC Day, Remembrance Day, and the ANZAC and Poppy Appeals. Launched Beyond the Barracks, the organisation's first podcast, and helped reposition its public voice following the Royal Commission into Defence and Veteran Suicide. The 2024 ANZAC Appeal grew online donations 35% year on year, including a 40% spike on launch day, and was shortlisted for a Fundraising Excellence Award by the Third Sector Awards.

CPA Australia · Editor, then Deputy Editor, 2019–2023

INTHEBLACK

Led editorial strategy and production for INTHEBLACK, CPA Australia's monthly business magazine (74–80 pages), plus a companion website, podcasts and video, delivering more than 400 pieces of content a year. Produced the 2020 mental health and wellbeing special edition, winner of a Mumbrella Publish Award in 2021, and the 2022 special edition on the future of accounting, CPA Australia's best-performing piece of content. INTHEBLACK's back catalogue is publicly viewable on CPA Australia's website.

Niche Media · Managing Editor, 2018

Custom client publications

Planned, commissioned, edited and proofread four custom publications: Business State for Bank of Melbourne, State of Play for Tabcorp, The Manual for the Australian Prostate Centre, and Grow for Rockend Technology, plus contributions to other titles covering tourism, marketing, architecture and design.

Moody's Analytics Knowledge Services · Senior Editor, 2012–2016

Investment research and internal communications

Content review and editing for research produced for global investment banks and research firms, including Deutsche Bank, ING and Société Générale, plus internal communications: RFPs, whitepapers, CVs, talent books and webinar content. Built and ran a new writing and editorial training program for analysts.

The Sunday Times · Features Journalist, 2007–2012

Long-form features and investigations

Idea generation and reporting for long-form features and investigations, human-interest interviews, and coverage of environmental issues, art, architecture, theatre and music.
